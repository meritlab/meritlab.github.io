MERIT Lab: Management of Energy Resources via Intelligent Techniques

MERIT Lab at Gachon University researches optimization, demand response, reinforcement learning, and data-driven control for intelligent energy resource management in smart grids, DERs, ESS, and distributed energy systems.

Core Research Areas

  • Optimization and resource management for DERs, ESS, and optimal scheduling
  • Demand side management, demand response, and data-driven consumption analysis
  • Machine learning and reinforcement learning for uncertain energy systems

Principal Investigator

Prof. Eunsung Oh is an Associate Professor at Gachon University working on energy resource optimization, demand management, and reinforcement-learning-based control.
