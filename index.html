MERIT Lab: Intelligent Energy Management for Flexible, Data-Driven Power Systems

MERIT Lab studies how distributed energy resources, storage, buildings, and demand-side flexibility should be scheduled, coordinated, and controlled when uncertainty, forecasting, and market participation all matter at the same time.

The interactive site loads with JavaScript. This summary gives visitors and search engines immediate access to the lab’s research direction, principal investigator, funded projects, and contact information.

Research Focus

  • Optimization and scheduling for DERs, ESS, and integrated energy systems
  • Demand response, demand flexibility, and grid-interactive buildings
  • Machine learning and reinforcement learning for uncertain energy systems
